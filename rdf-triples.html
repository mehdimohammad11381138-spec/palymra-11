RDF Triples & Enrichment

New RDF Triples Created

This section presents new RDF triples created to enrich the knowledge graph with information about Palmyra's authenticity, restoration methods, and post-conflict heritage status.

Vocabulary Extension

@prefix ex: <http://example.org/palmyra/> .

# New properties for authenticity and restoration
ex:restorationMethod "The method used to restore a cultural property" .
ex:authenticityStatus "The current authenticity status" .
ex:restorationActor "An organization involved in restoration" .
ex:materialAuthenticity "Assessment of physical material integrity" .

Temple of Bel Enrichment

Visual Proof: RDF Turtle Encoding

Figure 10: Actual RDF triples created in Turtle syntax, linking the monument to restoration and authenticity properties.

wd:Q324314 rdfs:label "Temple of Bel" ;
           ex:restorationMethod "Anastylosis" ;
           ex:authenticityStatus "Composite Authenticity" ;
           ex:materialAuthenticity "High - Original fragments reassembled" ;
           ex:restorationActor wd:Q1234 ;
           ex:restorationStartDate "2016-01-01"^^xsd:date ;
           ex:restorationEndDate "2019-12-31"^^xsd:date .
